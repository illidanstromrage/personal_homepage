As the eldest members in the Pac-12 league, we had a long tradition of playing one of them each year in their territory. In supporting our team, the band got this chance and called annual San Francisco trip as the "weekender". As we migrated to Big-10 and they joined in ACC starting this season, last year's game at Cal was our last ever "weekender" trip. The atmosphere got hyped as our team fought to the very last second to secure our win. And with this sweet victory and our huge home win against Standford earlier last season, these over a-hundred-year rivaries reached to the end.

This was two years ago when we were at Standford. We had a really bad season in the previous year, and this was the game to test our new head coach and quarterback. It was a huge win, marking the start of our conquering season. At the end of the season we finished 11-2, and brought home the 8th Heisman trophy, which made USC become having the most Heisman winners college.
This was during the postgame of last year's win over Cal. We played one of our classic rock charts: Animal House as the ending of our postgame performance. This move of us lying down and puting our feet in the air is the most astonishing part of this music. We usually don't play this chart at an away game, but we did this as the atmosphere got fied up with the thrilling ending of this game.
